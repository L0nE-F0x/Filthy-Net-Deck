Coverage

Two formats. Done properly.

No Alchemy. No rebalanced digital-only cards. Just the two constructed formats where the paper metagame is real, public, and trackable — covered with verified lists only.

The data promise: every list in the app is a real, current build from the live metagame. Every card name is checked against Scryfall before it ships. If a deck can’t be verified, it doesn’t appear — the app never shows placeholder, AI-guessed, or “seeded” decks.
